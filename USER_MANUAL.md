## System Overview

The **DICT Monday Flag Raising Attendance and Compliance Checking System** is a web-based platform designed to streamline and modernize the monitoring of employee attendance and compliance during the weekly flag-raising ceremony.

### Key Objectives
- **Improve Accuracy**: Digital automation minimizes manual processes and reduces errors
- **Enhance Efficiency**: Real-time data tracking and reporting for administrators
- **Promote Accountability**: Organized record-keeping to evaluate compliance over time
- **Support Government Operations**: Leverage technology to enhance accountability and discipline

### System Highlights
- Automated attendance check-in with camera capture (3-second auto-capture)
- Role-based access control (Admin and Employee roles)
- Compliance tracking and monitoring
- Real-time dashboard with recent attendance records
- Employee database management
- Area and department-based filtering

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Open your web browser and navigate to the system's URL
2. On the login page, note the live clock displaying the current time and date
3. Enter your **Username** and **Password** on the login page (use the eye icon to toggle password visibility)
4. Click **Login**

### Initial Login
When you first access the system, you will be assigned a role:
- **Admin**: Full system access and employee management capabilities
- **Employee**: Limited access to personal attendance records and check-in

### Changing Your Account Settings
After logging in, users can update their account information:
1. Navigate to your account settings page
2. Update your **Username**, **Email**, or **Password** as needed
3. For password changes, enter your current password and the new password (minimum 8 characters)
4. Click "Save" to apply changes

### Forgot Password
If you forget your password:
1. On the login page, click the "Forgot Password" link
2. Enter your email address associated with your account
3. Check your email for a password reset link
4. Follow the link to reset your password

## Admin Features

### 1. Dashboard

The Admin Dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of the system:

#### What You'll See:
- **Compliance Status Overview**: Visual representation of attendees vs. non-attendees
- **Recent Attendance Records**: Latest check-ins from employees
- **Compliance Filters**: Filter by:
  - Compliant/Non-compliant employees
  - Area of Assignment
  - Department
  - Status

#### How to Use:
1. Login as an Admin
2. You'll be automatically directed to the Dashboard
3. Use dropdown filters to view specific employee groups
4. Click "Compliant" or "Non-Compliant" tabs to toggle status views

### 2. Employee Management

Access the **Employee Management** section to manage your employee database.

#### Adding a New Employee

1. Navigate to **Employee Management**
2. Click **"Add New Employee"** button
3. Fill in the employee details:
   - **Full Name**: Employee's complete name
   - **Employee ID**: Unique identifier
   - **Department**: Department assignment
   - **Area of Assignment**: Work area/location
   - **Status**: Active/Inactive
   - **Email**: Contact email
   - (Additional fields as required)
4. Click **"Save"** to add the employee

#### Editing Employee Information

1. In **Employee Management**, find the employee in the data table
2. Click the **"Edit"** icon/button next to their name
3. Update the required information
4. Click **"Save Changes"**

#### Deleting an Employee

1. In **Employee Management**, find the employee
2. Click the **"Delete"** icon/button
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted
4. The employee record will be removed from the system

#### Filtering Employees

Use the filter options at the top of the Employee Management page:
- **Department Filter**: View employees by department
- **Status Filter**: View Active/Inactive employees
- **Role Filter**: View employees by their system role

#### Searching Employees

1. Use the **Search Bar** in the Employee Management table
2. Type the employee's name or ID
3. Results will filter in real-time

---

### 3. Attendance & Compliance Checking

Monitor attendance and compliance records for Monday flag-raising ceremonies.

#### Viewing Compliance Records

1. Navigate to **Dashboard** or **Compliance Checker**
2. Select the date (defaults to the current or most recent Monday)
3. Toggle between:
   - **Compliant Employees**: Those who attended
   - **Non-Compliant Employees**: Those who didn't attend
4. Filter by:
   - **Area of Assignment**: Select a specific work area
   - **Department**: Select a department
5. View the list with timestamps of when they checked in

#### Understanding Compliance Status

- **Compliant (✓)**: Employee checked in on time
- **Non-Compliant (✗)**: Employee did not check in or was late
- **Time Recorded**: Exact date and time of attendance

---

### 4. Reports and Records

#### Viewing Attendance Records

1. Go to **View Attendance** or **Recent Records**
2. You'll see a table with:
   - Employee name
   - Date of attendance
   - Time recorded
   - Compliance status
3. Use filters to narrow down results:
   - Date range
   - Department
   - Employee ID

#### Exporting Data

1. In any data table, look for the **Export** button (if available)
2. Select your preferred format:
   - **CSV**: Comma-separated values for Excel
   - **PDF**: For printing and archiving
   - **Excel**: Direct Excel format
3. The file will download automatically

---

### 5. Unlocking Attendance Dates

In some cases, you may need to unlock previous attendance dates for editing or re-submission.

#### How to Unlock Dates

1. Navigate to **Unlock Dates**
2. Select the date range you want to unlock
3. Provide a reason for unlocking
4. Click **"Unlock"**
5. Employees will now be able to update their attendance for that date

**Note**: Use this feature sparingly and only when necessary for system corrections.

## Employee Features

### 1. Personal Attendance Check-In

Employees can check in for the Monday flag-raising ceremony using the auto-capture feature.

#### To Check In:

1. Login as an Employee
2. Navigate to **"My Attendance"** or **"Check-In"**
3. Click **"Start Camera"** or **"Take Photo"**
4. Position your face in the camera frame
5. The system will **auto-capture your photo in 3 seconds**
6. Review your captured image in the preview modal
7. If satisfied, click **"Submit"**
8. If not satisfied, click **"Recapture"** to try again

#### Camera Tips:
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Face the camera directly
- Keep steadily in frame
- Remove sunglasses or hats if possible
- Ensure the preview shows you clearly before submitting

---

### 2. Viewing Your Attendance History

#### To View Your Attendance:

1. Navigate to **"My Attendance"** or **"View Attendance"**

   - **Time Recorded**: Your check-in time
   - **Status**: Compliant or Non-Compliant
3. Use filters if available to narrow down by date range

### 3. Personal Profile

2. Click **"Edit Profile"**
3. Update your information:
   - Full Name (if allowed)

   - Department (if changeable)
4. Click **"Save"**

## Common Tasks

### Task 1: Add a New Employee and Create User Account

**As Admin:**

1. Go to **Employee Management** → **Add New Employee**
2. Fill in all required fields
3. Click **"Save"**
4. Go to **Add New User** or **User Management**
5. Create a login account:
   - **Username**: Unique identifier
   - **Password**: Secure password
   - **Role**: Select "user" for regular employees
   - **Full Name**: Link to the employee record
   - **Employee ID**: Match the employee ID
6. Click **"Create Account"**

### Task 2: Check Attendance for a Specific Monday

**As Admin:**

1. Go to **Dashboard**
2. The system defaults to the current or most recent Monday
3. If needed, manually select the date
4. View the **"Recent Attendance Records"** section
5. Filter by area or department as needed
6. Review who attended and who didn't

### Task 3: Generate a Compliance Report

**As Admin:**

1. Go to **View Attendance** or **Reports**
2. Set your date range
3. Filter by department or area if needed
4. If available, click **"Export as PDF"** or **"Export as Excel"**
5. The report will be ready for download or printing

### Task 4: Employee Marks Attendance

**As Employee:**

1. Login to the system
2. Click **"My Attendance"** or **"Check-In"**
3. Follow the camera check-in process:
   - Click **"Start Camera"**
   - Wait for 3-second auto-capture
   - Review the photo preview
   - Click **"Submit"** to confirm
4. You'll receive a confirmation message
5. Your attendance will now appear on the admin dashboard

### Task 5: Mark Employee as Non-Compliant (Manually)

**As Admin:**

If an employee fails to check in through the system:

1. Go to the **Dashboard** or **Compliance Checker**
2. Find the employee in the list
3. Verify they didn't check in
4. The system will automatically mark them as "Non-Compliant" for that date
5. The record is logged for future reference

## Troubleshooting

### Camera Not Working

**Problem**: Camera won't start or freezes

**Solutions**:
1. Check browser permissions:
   - Allow the website to access your camera
   - Refresh the page
2. Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge)
3. Restart your device
4. Check your camera is not being used by another application
5. Clear your browser cache and cookies

### Login Issues

**Problem**: Can't login to the system

**Solutions**:
1. Verify your username and password are correct
2. Ensure CAPS LOCK is off
3. Clear browser cookies and cache
4. Try logging in from a different browser
5. Reset your password if you've forgotten it
6. Contact your admin for account troubleshooting

### Photo Quality Issues

**Problem**: Photo is blurry or pixelated

**Solutions**:
1. Improve lighting in your area
2. Position camera closer to your face (but keep it in frame)
3. Clean your camera lens
4. Try a different device
5. Use the **"Recapture"** button to take another photo

### Attendance Not Showing

**Problem**: Checked in but attendance doesn't appear

**Solutions**:
1. Refresh the page and check again
2. Wait a few seconds - there may be a delay
3. Check if you submitted the form completely
4. Look at the confirmation message
5. Contact admin to verify the record was saved

### Time Records Are Wrong

**Problem**: The system shows wrong time for attendance

**Solutions**:
1. Check that your device time is correct
2. Verify the system server time matches your time zone
3. Contact admin - there may be a server-side issue
4. Request admin to manually correct the record

### Access Denied Errors

**Problem**: Can't access certain pages

**Solutions**:
1. Verify you have the correct role (Admin vs. Employee)
2. Try logging out and logging back in
3. Clear browser cache
4. Contact admin if you need access to restricted areas
